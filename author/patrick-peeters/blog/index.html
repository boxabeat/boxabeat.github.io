Brasserie Croque-Notes

Fri 15 December 2023

The musicians of Brasserie Croque-Notes have been playing in various formations for many years, and singer Jo Vermeulen has been equally fascinated by the music and lyrics of Georges Brassens. The musicians and singer came together in 2003 at a party at ‘Café Local.’ Since then, they have occasionally performed some songs by Brassens – in private settings, during a French evening, or in a musical pub.

Capturing Musical Magic: Recordings with Eric De Vos

Wed 08 November 2023

Eric De Vos, a talented songwriter-composer-arranger and guitarist, has embarked on a musical adventure, crafting a collection of 7 captivating songs with the assistance of professional session musicians. These songs are born from the essence of his home-recorded pre-production demos, a labor of love that promised an eclectic auditory experience right from the start of our collaboration.

Lizard Smile

Wed 08 November 2023

Join us behind the scenes of Lizard Smile’s highly anticipated new album. Meet the trio from Antwerp, Belgium, known as Beatnick (Nico Van Aerde), the mesmerizing voice and master of programming; Strobo (Bart Van Laeken), the dream weaver on guitars and Albi (Albert Van Onckelen), the solid anchor on bass. Together, they form the enigmatic Electro Rock band, Lizard Smile.

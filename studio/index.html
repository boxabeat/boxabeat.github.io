Studio equipment

The studio is running on a MacPro with external hard disc Promise RAID6, 20 TB, a Lacie back-up 16 Tb and Logic Pro X. We have an Apogee Symphony I/O 24 channel AD-DA converter, with Thunderbridge. For demo recordings we provide a Presonus StudioLive 16.4.2 digital console in our basement studio with build-in pre-amps and converters to a MacBook Pro via Firewire. We have a top-level collection of pre-amps, sound processing gear, monitors, a 40-piece microphone set, etc. Scroll down to our gallery to get a visual glimpse of what we offer, or visit our recordings page to for some listening examples!
